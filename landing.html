Myanmar

I was born and raised in Myanmar (Burma) which is also known as the Land of a thousand pagodas. I came to the United States of America after completing high school in Myanmar. Learn more about Myanmar.

Computer and Internet

I did not have computer and internet access until I came to US. Since I left the country, there has been a significant growth of internet access in Myanmar and it helps people to stay connected to the world.

TV and Video Games

I grew up watching Tom and Jerry cartoons played on the government TV channel for about one hour at my grandmother's house on Sundays. Owning a TV back in the days in Myanmar was a luxury and fortunately, I did not have it. As a result, I spent most of my time playing with my cousins on the street. It was more fun than sitting in front of a TV. I have not owned a TV for the past 6+ years and do not miss it.

I tried playing video games and found it extremely boring.

Food

My all-time favourite cusine is Burmese and I love the traditional tea leaf salad. According to my friends and family, I am a good cook. I like hot and spicy food but never really like dessert, candy or chocolate. Besides water, Cardamom Chai and Latte, I do not usually drink anything else.
